**2nd-level abjuration**

**Casting Time**: 1 hour

**Range**: Touch

**Components**: V, S, M (bandages and poultices worth 50 gp, which the spell consumes)

**Duration**: Instantaneous

This spell can heal a number of minor injuries. It also restores 4d8 hit points.
