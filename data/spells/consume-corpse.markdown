**2nd-level necromancy**

**Casting Time**: 1 action

**Range**: Touch

**Components**: V, S

**Duration**: 1 hour

You touch a body that has been dead less than 10 minutes and absorb the trace life essence from it. The corpse rots until all that remains is bones. You gain temporary hitpoints equal to 3d8 + your spellcasting modifier. After the duration expires you must make a constitution save against your own spell save DC or become sickened for 10 minutes. The body you touch must be of one of the following types: humanoid, beast, fey, monstrosity, giant, dragon, or aberration and must be of size large or smaller.

**At Higher Levels:** For each level above second you gain an additional 1d8 temporary hitpoints and can drain a corpse of an additional size category larger.
