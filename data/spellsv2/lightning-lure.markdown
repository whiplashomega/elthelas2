**Evocation cantrip**

**Casting Time**: 1 action

**Range**: Self (4m radius)

**Components**: V

**Duration**: Instantaneous

**Saving Throw**: Strength

**Save For**: not pulled

**Damage**: [ { "dice": "1d8", "label": "lightning", "damagebonus": 0, "addstat": false, "prof": false, "higherlevels": "" } ]

You create a lash of lightning energy that strikes at one creature of your choice that you can see within 4m of you. The target must succeed on a Strength saving throw or be pulled up to 3m in a straight line toward you and then take 1d8 lightning damage if it is within 1m of you.

This spell's damage increases by 1d8 when you reach 5th level (2d8), 11th level (3d8), and 17th level (4d8).
